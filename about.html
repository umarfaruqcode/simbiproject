About Us

SLUM AND RURAL HEALTH INITIATIVE NETWORK.

We are a non-profit organization that is focused on delivering quality healthcare information and services to people in rural areas, urban slums and other under-served areas through community outreaches, media advocacy, information technology, research advocacy and other innovative approaches to connect healthcare professionals to the populace and policymakers to ensure that quality healthcare is available to everyone, everywhere.
